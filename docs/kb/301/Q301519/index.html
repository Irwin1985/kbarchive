Q301519: Customer receives e-mail at username@hotmail.msn.com but not at

Article: Q301519
Product(s): The Microsoft Network
Version(s): 6.1
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 20-AUG-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- The Microsoft Network version 6.1 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

You receive e-mail messages sent to username@hotmail.msn.com, but do not receive
e-mail messages sent to username@msn.com.

CAUSE
=====

There is an error in the routing table on the your account.

RESOLUTION
==========

Please contact technical support for resolution.

Additional query words: kbimu MSN Explorer
