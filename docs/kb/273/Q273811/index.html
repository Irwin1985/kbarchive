Q273811: AoE II Expansion: The Conquerors: Minimum System Requirements

Article: Q273811
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): 2.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kbref kbimu msgame
Last Modified: 22-FEB-2002

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, version 2.0 
- Microsoft Age of Empires II Expansion: The Conquerors 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article contains a list of the minimum system requirements needed to play
both Microsoft Age of Empires II Expansion: The Conquerors, and Microsoft Age of
Empires II: The Age of Kings.

NOTE: To play The Age of Empires II: The Conquerors you must have The Age of
Empires II: The Age of Kings installed.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To play either of these games you need:

- Multimedia PC with Pentium 166 MHz or higher processor

- Microsoft Windows 95 or later operating system, Microsoft Windows NT
  Workstation operating system version 4.0 with Service Pack 5 or later, or
  Microsoft Windows 2000 Professional operating system

- 32 megabytes (MB) of RAM for Windows 95, Windows 98, or Windows Millennium
  Edition; 64 MB for Windows NT or Windows 2000

- 100 MB of available hard disk space (an additional 100 MB of available hard
  disk space is required for the swap file)

- Super VGA monitor capable of displaying 16-bit color at a screen resolution
  of 800 x 600 pixels

- Local bus video adapter with 2 MB of video memory capable of displaying
  16-bit color at a screen resolution of 800 x 600 pixels

- Quad-speed or faster CD-ROM drive

- Microsoft Mouse or compatible pointing device

- Sound card with speakers or headphones for audio

Requirements for multiplayer play:

- 28,800 bits-per-second (bps) or faster modem for Internet or head-to-head
  play

- Internet access required for Internet play. May require payment of a separate
  fee to an Internet service provider (ISP). Connect time charges may apply.

- To play on Zone.com, you need to have one of the following Web browsers:
  Microsoft Internet Explorer 3.02 or later or Netscape Communicator 4.0 or
  later
