Q192507: Microsoft Word 97 At A Glance Comments and Corrections

Article: Q192507
Product(s): Microsoft Press
Version(s): 
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kbdocerr
Last Modified: 08-JAN-2000

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- MSPRESS Microsoft Word 97 At A Glance ISBN 1-57231-366-8 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
relating to the Microsoft Press book "Microsoft Word 97 At A Glance," ISBN
1-57231-366-8.

The following topics are covered:

- Page 54 and 55: Incorrect Icons

MORE INFORMATION
================

Page 54 and 55: Incorrect Icons
-------------------------------

Page 54 and 55:
The icons for Page Layout View and Online Layout View need to be swapped.


Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
already contain most or all of the above corrections.
