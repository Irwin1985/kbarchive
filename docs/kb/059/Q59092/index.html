Q59092: Oakland Runway Disappears on Landing Approach

Article: Q59092
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): 
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 30-JUL-2001

SUMMARY
=======

While attempting a landing at Oakland International Airport runway 15/33, the
runway reportedly disappears when the aircraft gets within an altitude of 100
feet above the runway.

Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here as it
becomes available.
