Q244247: Age of Empires II: List of Campaigns and Scenarios

Article: Q244247
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): WINDOWS:2.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): aoe kbimu msgame
Last Modified: 12-JUN-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, version 2.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings includes scenarios for five new
campaigns you can play in single-player mode. You can choose to play these
scenarios in five increasing levels of difficulty, ranging from Easiest to
Hardest.

MORE INFORMATION
================

The following list contains the campaigns and associated scenarios that are
included in Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings.

William Wallace: Learning Campaign
----------------------------------

1. Marching and Fighting
2. Feeding the Army
3. Training the Troops
4. Research and Technology
5. The Battle of Stirling
6. Forge an Alliance
7. The Battle of Falkirk

Joan of Arc
-----------

1. An Unlikely Messiah
2. The Maid of Orleans
3. The Cleansing of the Loire
4. The Rising
5. The Siege of Paris
6. The Perfect Martyr

Saladin
-------

1. An Arabian Knight
2. Lord of Arabia
3. The Horns of Hattin
4. The Siege of Jerusalem
5. Jihad!
6. The Lion and the Demon

Genghis Khan
------------

1. Crucible
2. A Life of Revenge
3. Into China
4. The Horde Rides West
5. The Promise
6. Pax Mongolia

Barbarossa
----------

1. Holy Roman Emperor
2. Henry the Lion
3. Pope and Antipope
4. The Lombard League
5. Barbarossa's March
6. The Emperor Sleeping
