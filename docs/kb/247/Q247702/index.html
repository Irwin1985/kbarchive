Q247702: Fighter Ace II: Gun Muzzle Flashes Appear Blocky

Article: Q247702
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): 
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay igz kbimu msgame
Last Modified: 09-NOV-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Fighter Ace II 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft Fighter Ace II, the gun muzzle flashes may appear
blocky.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the video adapter installed in your computer is based
on the Rendition Verite 2100 chip set.

The blending effects required for Fighter Ace II to run properly are not
supported by the Rendition Verite 2100 chip set.

================
