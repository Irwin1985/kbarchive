Q272538: Summary of Online Help for MSN Companion

Article: Q272538
Product(s): The Microsoft Network
Version(s): 1.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- MSN Companion, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

Simple directions for a variety of help resources for MSN Companion.

MORE INFORMATION
================

If you have a question about your MSN Companion, the first thing to do is go the
the Start page and click Help. This brings you to the help center where a
variety of help is available.

Available Help Sections:

- The help contents section lists a variety of help topics and available
  "tours." Tours are Flash animations that provide a step-by-step guide to many
  MSN Web Companion features.

- The announcements section provide information from Microsoft about upgrades,
  outages, promotions, and so forth. A link to Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
  for MSN Web Companion is also found here.

- The member services section provides account information, information about
  MSN Companion, Passport, MSN, and links to customer and technical support.

- The safety and privacy sectioin addresses customer concerns on these issues.
