Q272342: Plus! Game Pack: No Game Sounds with Sound Blaster 16 CT 2950

Article: Q272342
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0; :
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbsound kbimu msgame
Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Entertainment Pack: The Puzzle Collection, version 1.0 
- Microsoft Plus! Game Pack: Cards and Puzzles 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play a game in one of the programs listed at the beginning of this
article, sounds in the game may not be played.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if a Creative Labs Sound Blaster 16 (CT 2950) sound card
is installed in your computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

There is no resolution to this issue at this time.

The third-party products discussed in this article are manufactured by vendors
independent of Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding
these products' performance or reliability.
