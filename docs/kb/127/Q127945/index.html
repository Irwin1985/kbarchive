Q127945: Printers Supported by PSMON.DLL

Article: Q127945
Product(s): Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
Version(s): 
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 17-DEC-2000

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Windows 95 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article lists the PostScript printers that are supported for back- channel
messaging in PSMON.DLL.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Back-channel messaging is used to supply the information displayed by the print
monitor during printing. This information includes printer status information
such as "Paused" or "User intervention required."

The following PostScript printers are supported by PSMON.DLL for back- channel
messaging:

  APLW8101.PPD   Apple LaserWriter Pro 810
  APLW81F1.PPD   Apple LaserWriter Pro 810 with Fax Card
  DC5100_1.PPD   Digital DEClaser 5100
  HP650_C1.PPD   Hewlett-Packard (HP) DesignJet 650C
  HP1200C1.PPD   HP DeskJet 1200C/PS
  HP4M3_V1.PPD   HP LaserJet 4
  HP4M6_v1.PPD   HP LaserJet 4
  HP4ML_V1.PPD   HP LasetJet 4ML
  HP4MP6_1.PPD   HP LaserJet 4MP
  HP4SI3_1.PPD   HP LasetJet 4Si/4SiMX PS 300dpi
  HP4SI6_1.PPD   HP LaserJet 4Si/4SiMX PS 600dpi
  HPPJXL32.PPD   XP PaintJet XL300
  TIL23521.PPD   Texas Instruments (TI) MicroLaser Pro 600 2013 PS23
  TIL65521.PPD   TI MicroLaser Pro 600 2013 PS65

Additional query words: back channel
