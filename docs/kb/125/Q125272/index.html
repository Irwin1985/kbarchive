Q125272: Determining Whether NEC Monitors Are Energy Star Compliant

Article: Q125272
Product(s): Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
Version(s): 
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): win95
Last Modified: 07-FEB-2002

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Windows 95 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

Some NEC monitors are compliant with the Energy Star specifications. These
monitors include the FGe and V series, with the exception of the 3FGe model. To
determine if the monitor in question is Energy Star compliant, verify that the
model number for the monitor ends in "-03". If the monitor model number does not
end in "-03", the monitor is not compliant.

or reliability.
