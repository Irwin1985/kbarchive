Q125312: Cinemania 1995: Wrong Listing for Steve Martin Filmography

Article: Q125312
Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
Version(s): 1995 edition
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 11-DEC-1999

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Cinemania for Windows 1995 edition 
- Microsoft Cinemania for the Macintosh 1995 edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The Filmography section of the Steve Martin entry of Cinemania 1995 incorrectly
lists the 1950 version of the film "Father of the Bride."

As the Biography section correctly notes, Steve Martin was in the 1991 film of
the same name.

This issue was corrected in the 1996 edition of Cinemania.
