Q110880: Encarta 94: Copyright Information Doesn’t Paste Into Write

Article: Q110880
Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
Version(s): 1994 editions
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 26-MAY-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Encarta 1994 The Complete Multimedia Encyclopedia 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Page 15 of the "Microsoft Encarta To the Finish Line!" booklet discusses
copying and pasting a portion of text into Write and automatically
inserting copyright information. The guide states the following:

  Did it work? You should see the paragraph from "Mask" added to the bottom of
  your report. Notice that the copyright information is added automatically.

However, the copyright information will not appear unless the ENTIRE text
is copied. Copying part of the text will not allow the copyright
information to be pasted into your word processor.
