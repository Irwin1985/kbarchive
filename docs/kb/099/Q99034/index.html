Q99034: SONET Signal Rates

Article: Q99034
Product(s): Microsoft LAN Manager
Version(s): 
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 30-JUL-2001

SUMMARY
=======

This is the list of some common sonet signal rates:

                     SONET line rates
---------------------------------------------------------------
Electrical Optical                      Equivalent   Equivalent
signal     equivalent  Line rate        DS1s         DS3s
---------------------------------------------------------------

STS1      OC1         51.840 Mbps       28           1
---------------------------------------------------------------
STS3      OC3         155.520 Mbps      84           3
---------------------------------------------------------------
          OC9         466.560 Mbps      252          9
---------------------------------------------------------------
          OC12        622.080 Mbps      356          12
---------------------------------------------------------------
          OC18        933.120 Mbps      504          18
---------------------------------------------------------------
          OC24        1.244 Gbps        572          24
---------------------------------------------------------------
          OC36        1.866 Gbps        1008         36
---------------------------------------------------------------
          OC48        2.488 Gbps        1344         48
---------------------------------------------------------------
STS: Synchronous Transport Signal
OC:  Optical Carrier
