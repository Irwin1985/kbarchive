Q175153: FS98: How To Print Airport Directories and Navaids Lists

Article: Q175153
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): WINDOWS:
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kbprint kbui fs98 kbimu msgame
Last Modified: 07-MAY-2002

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Flight Simulator 98 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article describes how to print airport directories or Navaid listings from
Flight Simulator 98.

To do this, copy the information from Flight Simulator 98, and then paste the
text into a word processing program such as WordPad or Microsoft Word.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To use use WordPad to print airport directories or Navaid listings:

1. On the World menu, click Airport/Facility Directory.

2. On the global map, click the global region in which you are interested.

3. On the regional map, click the specific region, state, or province in which
  you are interested.

4. Click Airports or Navaids.

5. Click anywhere in the list of Airports or Navaids, and then press CTRL+A to
  select the entire list.

6. Press CTRL+C to copy the list.

7. Click Start, point to Programs, point to Accessories, and then click WordPad.

8. On the Edit menu in WordPad, click Paste.

9. On the File menu, click Print.

10. On the File menu, click Exit. When you are prompted to save the changes,
  click Yes or No.
