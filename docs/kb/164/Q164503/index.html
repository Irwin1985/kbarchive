Q164503: WD97: How to Order the Resource and Developer’s Kits

Article: Q164503
Product(s): Word 97 for Windows
Version(s): 
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): word97
Last Modified: 20-FEB-2002

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Word 97 for Windows 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


SUMMARY
=======

The Microsoft Word Developer's Kit for Word 97 has been combined with the other
Office 97 Developer's kits and is available in the following product:

  The Microsoft Office 97, Developer Edition Professional (ODE)
  Part Number: 353-00005
  Price : $599.0 U.S.

Other developer materials include:

  The Microsoft Office 97, Developer Edition Professional Upgrade (ODE)
  Part Number: 053-00014
  Price : $499.0 U.S.

  The Microsoft Office 97, Developer Edition Standard (ODE)
  Part Number: 021-00691
  Price : $499.0 U.S.

  The Microsoft Office 97, Developer Edition Standard Upgrade (ODE)
  Part Number: 021-00703
  Price : $249.0 U.S.

The Microsoft Word Resource Kit for Word 97 has been combined with the other
Office 97 resource kits into a kit called:

  The Microsoft Office 97 Resource Kit (ORK)
  Author: Microsoft Corporation
  ISBN : 1-57231-329-3
  Pages : 1184
  Disk : 1 CD-ROM
  Price : $59.99 U.S.
  Level : Intermediate/Advanced

MORE INFORMATION
================

Microsoft Office 97, Developer Edition Professional (ODE)
---------------------------------------------------------

The newest version of Microsoft Office has everything for building and
distributing solutions with Microsoft Office 97. Office 97, Developer Edition is
designed for developers creating solutions with Microsoft Office. It includes:

1. Microsoft Office 97, Professional Edition.

2. Microsoft Office 97 Developer Edition Tools:

   - Royalty-free Microsoft run-time license.

   - Microsoft Access/Microsoft Visual SourceSafe(TM) integration tools for
     team development.

   - Replication Manager for viewing and managing replicated databases on a
     network or the Internet.

   - Setup Wizard for creating familiar setup routines that work like Microsoft
     Office setup.

   - Additional ActiveX controls, including FTP, HTTP, and Gopher.

3. Printed developer documentation:

   - Office 97/Visual Basic Programmer's Guide, 496 pages.

   - Building Applications with Microsoft Access 97, 678 pages.

   - Microsoft Office 97 Object Model Guide, 33 pages.

4. Technical information and code samples from the Microsoft Developer Network.

To visit the Microsoft Office 97, Developer Edition, Web site, connect to:

  http://www.microsoft.com/AccessDev/

To order Microsoft Office 97, Developer Edition Development, in the United States
or Canada, call (800) 440-5079. Outside the United States and Canada, please
contact your local Microsoft subsidiary.

For additional information, please see the following article in the Microsoft
Knowledge Base:

  Q164504 System Requirements for MS Office 97 Developer Edition

The Microsoft Office 97 Resource Kit
------------------------------------

If you're an administrator or an IS professional, this book was written for you.
Here you'll find the hands-on, in-depth information you need to roll out,
support, and troubleshoot Microsoft Office 97. For systems running Microsoft
Windows 95 and Microsoft Windows NT (3.51 and 4.0), this is the professional's
guide to Microsoft Office 97. Here's hands-on information about updating from
earlier versions of Microsoft Office, about switching from other applications
such as Lotus 1-2-3 and WordPerfect, and about coexistence among different
versions of Microsoft Office. Of course, you get full information on network
installation, plus the timesaving Network Installation Wizard on the enclosed
CD-ROM. And like all the tools and utilities on the CD-ROM, this wizard is a
tested, supported application designed to make your job easier.

To visit the Microsoft Office 97 Resource Kit Web site, connect to:

  http://www.microsoft.com/Office/ORK/

At this time we are only able to offer direct ordering for customers who live in
the United States and Canada, but we will soon offer worldwide direct ordering.
For all direct orders, Microsoft Press requires prepayment by check, money
order, or credit card in United States Dollars. MasterCard, VISA, and American
Express credit cards are accepted.

IMPORTANT THINGS TO KNOW WHEN YOU ORDER DIRECT
----------------------------------------------

When placing your order by any method, please provide the following information:

- Accurate book title, ISBN number, quantity, and total price, including
  shipping charges and applicable sales taxes. Shipping charges will be taxed
  if applicable.

- Your name, address (no P.O. boxes, please), city, state, country, ZIP or
  postal code, and daytime telephone number including area code (in case there
  is a problem with your order).

- Complete payment in United States dollars: check, money order, VISA,
  MasterCard, or American Express. Please include expiration date on all credit
  card orders.

Three Easy Ways To Order:

1. Order by Phone:

  From within the United States
  Call (800) MSPRESS or (800) 677-7377. Call Microsoft Press directly to place
  your order, 7 AM to 9 PM Central Time.

  From Canada
  Call (800) 667-1115 Monday through Friday, 8am to 4:30pm Eastern Time or from
  Toronto, call 1-416-293-8464.

2. Order by Mail: Send us your order in the mail.

Mail your order with check, money order or complete credit card information
including expiration date. Daytime telephone number is required on all mail
orders.

United States Destinations

3. Order by fax: Conveniently fax us your order, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

  Fax your order with complete credit card information including expiration
