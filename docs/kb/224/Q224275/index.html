Q224275: S&T 2000: Cannot Enter Latitude and Longitude in All Formats

Article: Q224275
Product(s): Microsoft Automap
Version(s): WINDOWS:
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kbui kbdocerr kbimu
Last Modified: 27-JUL-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Expedia Streets and Trips 2000 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

In Microsoft Expedia Streets and Trips 2000, the Latitude And Longitude online
Help topic incorrectly states that you can specify both latitude and longitude
coordinates in any of the following formats:

- Decimal degrees

- Degrees and minutes

- Degrees, minutes, and seconds

However, when you attempt to search for a specific location based on its latitude
and longitude (to do this, click Latitude/Longitude on the Find menu), you are
only able to enter the latitude and longitude coordinates in decimal degrees.

MORE INFORMATION
================

When you use the Location Sensor, you can display latitude and longitude
coordinates in any of the formats listed in the "Summary" section of this
article.
