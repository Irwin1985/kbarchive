Q129021: CINEMANIA: The Film Encyclopedia Not Included in Cinemania

Article: Q129021
Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
Version(s): MACINTOSH:; :1994 edition,1995, 1996, 1997 editions
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 11-DEC-1999

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Cinemania for Macintosh, 1994, 1995, 1996, 1997 editions 
- Microsoft Cinemania for the Macintosh versions 1994 edition, 1995, 1996, 1997 editions 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The entire contents of Ephraim Katz's "The Film Encyclopedia," are not included
in Microsoft Cinemania.

MORE INFORMATION
================

The Cinemania packaging advertises, "More reviews, cinema topics, and a glossary
of movie terms culled from Ephraim Katz's The Film Encyclopedia... ." This
statement does not mean the books entire contents is included in Cinemania.

Microsoft's licensing agreement with the encyclopedia's publisher, Harper
Collins, allows only about 1,000 entries to be included in Microsoft Cinemania.
These entries are primarily confined to biography and topical filmography.

"The Film Encyclopedia" is a comprehensive one-volume film encyclopedia
containing more than 7,000 entries on subjects such as:

- Directors, producers, actors, cinematographers, screenwriters, composers, and
  art directors.

- Motion picture studios and film centers.

- Film related organizations and events.

- Styles, genres, and schools of film making.

- Industry jargon and technical terms.

- Inventions, inventors, and equipment.
