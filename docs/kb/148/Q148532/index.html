Q148532: "Fury 3: The Official Strategy Guide" Book Available

Article: Q148532
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kbfaq
Last Modified: 08-NOV-2001

1.00

kbmm kbusage kbref kbfaq kbfasttip

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Fury 3 for Windows, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

A new book on hints, tips, strategies and cheat codes is available from Prima
Publishing.

The "Fury 3: The Official Strategy Guide" by Steve Poole, is a comprehensive
guide to playing Fury that includes a chapter for every level of the game. The
missions of each Planet are described in detail and include hints and tips to
survive and continue to the next challenging adventure.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Suggested retail price for guide is $19.95.

To order the book, contact Prima Publishing at (916) 632-4400, or contact your
local retailer. The ISBN is 0-7615-0389-7.
