Q150342: Explorapedia Nature: Topics Listed By Scene

Article: Q150342
Product(s): Microsoft Home Kids Products
Version(s): 1.0 1.1
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 23-OCT-1999

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Explorapedia series: World of Nature for Windows, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

To get a list of the scene topics in Explorapedia:

1. Click the scene you want to go to from the spaceship.

2. After you are in the scene, click Tad.

3. Click "See all of the topics that are in this scene."

MORE INFORMATION
================

Listed below are the topics found in Explorapedia, the World of Nature, broken
down by each screen you encounter outside of the spaceship. You can also click
the screen name for information about the type of area that you are in.
