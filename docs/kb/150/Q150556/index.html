Q150556: Cinemania 96 Update Contents: December 95

Article: Q150556
Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
Version(s): 1996 edition
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 16-DEC-1999

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Cinemania for Windows 1996 edition 
- Microsoft Cinemania for the Macintosh 1996 edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The following article lists the movie titles included in the December 95 update
of Cinemania 1996:

Movie Titles
------------

The American President - Carrington - Casino - Copycat - GoldenEye - Home for the
Holidays - It Takes Two - Kicking and Screaming - Leaving Las Vegas - Les
Miserables - Mighty Aphrodite - Nick of Time - Reckless - Something to Talk
About - Total Eclipse - When Night is Falling
