Q275868: CFS2: Tips and Suggestions for Training Missions

Article: Q275868
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): 1.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kbimu msgame
Last Modified: 07-MAY-2002

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator 2: WWII Pacific Theater, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article contains some helpful tips and suggestions for how to fly training
missions in Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator 2.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To successfully complete a training mission in Combat Flight Simulator 2:

- Follow the Instructor Carefully

  Some maneuvers may take longer than expected.

- Use the Default Aircraft to Fly Training Missions

  Aircraft characteristics may vary.

  The American default aircraft is the Corsair. The Japanese default aircraft is
  the Zero.

- Japanese Training Mission 05

  The text that explains that you must invert for the "Split S" maneuver does
  not appear until you complete the maneuver.

- Japanese Training Mission 06

  There is a conflict between two audio tracks. A report of bandits in your
  sector and the instructor's voice are played at the same time. The bandit
  report may not be audible.

- American and Japanese Training Mission 10

  The "Show Maneuver" video clip depicts the aircraft making an approach that
  does not reflect the description that is portrayed in the training mission.
  This training mission is a straight-in approach, not a traffic pattern
  approach.
