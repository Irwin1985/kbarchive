Q316103: MCSE Training Kit Microsoft BizTalk Server 2000 Comments And Cor

Article: Q316103
Product(s): Microsoft Press
Version(s): 
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kbdocfix
Last Modified: 28-JUN-2002

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- MSPRESS MCSE Training Kit Microsoft BizTalk Server 2000 ISBN 0-7356-1440-7 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
relating to the Microsoft Press book MCSE Training Kit Microsoft BizTalk Server
2000, ISBN 0-7356-1440-7.

The following topics are covered:

- Page 244: Corrections To Steps 4 and 7

- Page 246: Correction To Step 7

- Pages 310 And 601: IBTSCustom Should Be IBTSCustomProcess

- Page 341: Missing Shape

- Page 495: Correction To A Link

MORE INFORMATION
================

Page 244: Corrections To Steps 4 and 7
--------------------------------------

On page 244, steps 4 and 7 are incorrect.

Change:

"4. In the Select A Document Definition dialog box, click Customer Energy Usage
Summary, and then click OK.

7. In the Select A Document Definition dialog box, click Customer Energy Usage,
and then click OK."

To:
"4. In the Select A Document Definition dialog box, click Customer Energy Usage,
and then click OK.

7. In the Select A Document Definition dialog box, click Customer Energy Usage
Summary, and then click OK."


Page 246: Correction To Step 7
------------------------------

On page 246, in step 7, change:
"Customer Energy"

To:
"Customer Energy Usage Summary"


Pages 310 And 601: IBTSCustom Should Be IBTSCustomProcess
---------------------------------------------------------

On pages 310 and 601, in question 10,

Change:
"D. IBTSCustom"

To:
"D. IBTSCustomProcess"


Page 341: Missing Shape
-----------------------

On page 341, in the last paragraph,

Change:
"Orchestration designer uses nine Flowchart shapes: Begin, End, Abort, Decision,
While, Fork, Join and Transaction."

To:
"Orchestration designer uses nine Flowchart shapes: Action, Begin, End, Abort,
Decision, While, Fork, Join and Transaction."


Page 495: Correction To A Link
------------------------------

On page 495, in the third paragraph,

Change:
"http://microsoft.com/net/servers/aplicationcenter2000"

To:
"http://www.microsoft.com/applicationcenter/"


Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
already contain most or all of the above corrections.
