Q155351: Cinemania 96 Update Contents: August 96

Article: Q155351
Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
Version(s): 1996 edition
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 17-DEC-1999

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Cinemania for Windows 1996 edition 
- Microsoft Cinemania for the Macintosh 1996 edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The following article lists the movie titles included in the August 96 update of
Cinemania 1996:

Movie Titles
------------

The Adventures of Pinocchio (1996) - August (1996) - Basquiat (1996) - Celestial
Clockwork (1993) - Cold Fever (1994) - Courage Under Fire (1996) - Emma (1996) -
Fled (1996) - The Frighteners (1996) - Guimba the Tyrant (1995) - Harriet the
Spy (1996) - Heavy (1995) - Homage (1995) - House Arrest (1996) - Jack (1996) -
Joe's Apartment (1996) - Kazaam (1996) - Kingpin (1996) - Manny & Lo (1996)
- Matilda (1996) - Maybe ... Maybe Not (1994) - Me and My Matchmaker (1996) -
Multiplicity (1996) - Stonewall (1996) - Time To Kill, A (1996) - Trainspotting
(1996) - The Visitors (1993) - Walking and Talking (1996)
