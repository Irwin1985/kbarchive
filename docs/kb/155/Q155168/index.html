Q155168: Encarta 96: Contents of the May 1996 Yearbook Update

Article: Q155168
Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
Version(s): WINDOWS:; :1996 edition
Operating System(s):

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Encarta 96 Encyclopedia for Windows 
- Microsoft Encarta 96 Encyclopedia for Macintosh 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article lists the features and articles that are included in the May 1996
yearbook update for the Encarta Encyclopedia.

MORE INFORMATION
================

the May 1996 yearbook update file:
