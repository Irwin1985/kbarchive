Q100380: Pictures with More than 16 Colors Look Bad in JigSawed

Article: Q100380
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): WINDOWS:
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 11-JUN-1999

kbusage

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Windows Entertainment Pack, volume 2 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

Pictures with more than 16 colors will not display properly when used in the
JigSawed game that ships with Microsoft Entertainment Pack volume 2. JigSawed is
a 16-color application. Consequently, images used in JigSawed should have equal
to or less than 16 colors. Pictures with 256 colors or more will look
unpleasant.

Additional query words: 1.00 ugly 256 bad poor jig saw sawed jigsaw WEP3
