Q217579: Motocross Madness: Ground Textures Are Distorted or Missing

Article: Q217579
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): 1.0,2.1,2.5
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbenv kbimu msgame
Last Modified: 13-JUN-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Motocross Madness, version 1.0 
- the operating system: Microsoft Windows 95, versions 2.1, 2.5 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft Motocross Madness, ground textures may be distorted or
missing.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if all of the following conditions are true:

- Microsoft Windows 95 OEM Service Release 2.1 (OSR 2.1) or Microsoft Windows
  95 OEM Service Release 2.5 (OSR 2.5) is installed on your computer.

- A 3Dfx Voodoo-based video adapter is installed in your computer.

- The 3Dfx Voodoo video driver provided with Windows 95 OSR 2.1 or Windows 95
  OSR 2.5 is installed on your computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, contact your video adapter manufacturer to inquire about
how to obtain and install the latest version of the video driver for your video
adapter.

For more information about how to contact your video adapter manufacturer, please
see the following articles in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

  Q65416 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, A-K

  Q60781 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, L-P

  Q60782 Hardware and Software Third-Party Vendor Contact List, Q-Z

MORE INFORMATION
================

To determine the brand and model of your video adapter:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click System.

3. Click Device Manager.

4. Double-click Display Adapters.

In the branch under Display Adapters, the brand and model of your video adapter
is displayed.

5. Close Control Panel.
